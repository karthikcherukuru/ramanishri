## 1.2 Page priority & conversion role

| Tier | Pages | Role |
|---|---|---|
| **Primary** | Donate, Home | Drive donations (one-time + monthly) |
| **Secondary** | Volunteer, Celebrate With Ramanishri | Build active supporter base & peer fundraising |
| **Supporting / Trust** | About, Support & Prayers, Gallery | Credibility, emotional connection, transparency |
| **Utility** | Contact | Inquiries, partnerships, support routing |

## 1.3 Content design principles

1. **One primary action per screen** — never compete two CTAs of equal weight.
2. **Proof before ask** — impact numbers and real stories precede every donation request.
3. **Specific over vague** — "₹500 feeds a child for a week" beats "Your gift helps."
4. **Show the child, not the charity** — lead with beneficiaries, faces, outcomes.
5. **Transparency = conversion** — surface 80G, financials, and accountability everywhere.
6. **Mobile-first reading** — short paragraphs, scannable headings, thumb-reachable CTAs.
