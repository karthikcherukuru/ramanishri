Our Story

It Began With a Single Meal

Ramanishri Foundation was born in 2022 out of a refusal to look away. We saw orphaned children facing each day without the most basic certainties — a meal, a classroom, someone who cared.

So we started small: a plate of food, a kind word, a promise to come back tomorrow. That promise became our purpose.

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To uplift orphaned children by providing nourishment, education, care, and opportunities — restoring not just their wellbeing, but their hope for a brighter future.

Our Vision

A future in which every orphaned child is nourished, educated, protected, and empowered — free to dream without limits.
