Who We Are

A Family for Children Who Have None

Ramanishri Foundation was founded in 2022 with a single, urgent belief — no orphaned child should ever go hungry, unschooled, or unloved. We provide daily nourishment, access to education, healthcare, and the everyday care that helps vulnerable children grow into confident adults.

We don't just feed children; we walk beside them.

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To uplift orphaned children by providing nourishment, education, care, and opportunities — and with them, the hope of a brighter future.

Our Vision
